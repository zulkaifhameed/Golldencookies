Our Story

Born from a love of chocolate

Golldencookies began in a home kitchen, with one simple obsession — making the most indulgent, soul-warming cookie imaginable. Dark brown, thick-baked, loaded with chocolate chips.

We landed on two perfect flavours: Galaxy for the dreamy, silky-smooth chocolate lover, and Kinder for those who crave that classic hazelnut-cream magic. Every box comes with 9 freshly baked cookies.
